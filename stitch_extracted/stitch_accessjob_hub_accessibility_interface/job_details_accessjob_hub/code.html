Job Description

We are looking for a Senior Frontend Developer who is passionate about creating accessible, performant, and robust user interfaces. You will be instrumental in leading our transition to a strictly accessible design system, ensuring our products are usable by everyone.

Key Responsibilities

  • Architect and develop high-performance web applications using modern JavaScript frameworks (React, Vue, or Angular).
  • Lead internal accessibility audits and remediations to meet WCAG 2.2 AAA standards.
  • Collaborate closely with UI/UX designers to translate complex requirements into intuitive, accessible interfaces.
  • Mentor junior developers in inclusive design practices and semantic HTML.

Requirements

  • 5+ years of professional experience in frontend development.
  • Deep, demonstrable understanding of semantic HTML, ARIA roles, and keyboard navigation paradigms.
  • Experience with automated accessibility testing tools (axe-core, Lighthouse).
  • Strong proficiency in modern CSS (Grid, Flexbox, Custom Properties) and CSS-in-JS or Tailwind.
